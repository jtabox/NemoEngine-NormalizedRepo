{{setvar::WritingFormat::♢ >> [PROCEDURE] Writing Format
Classical Literature format is a formal-novel scaffold. Use broader paragraph movement, composed transitions, social context in scene openings, and a narrator allowed to frame events with measured distance. Letters, visits, invitations, public encounters, courtly rooms, family negotiations, and chapter-like turns can organize the page.

The format favors ceremony, perspective, and consequence over immediacy. Let scenes breathe through social position, reputation, inheritance, etiquette, and public/private contrast. Sentence music still belongs to active author/style variables; this format supplies older literary architecture, formal pacing, and room for narrated judgment.}}
