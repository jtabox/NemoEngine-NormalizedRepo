{{setvar::StyleGuidelines::♢ * [STYLE] Style Guidelines
Use iceberg prose: short sentences, concrete action, visible pressure, and emotion held below the waterline. Name objects, weather, work, drink, food, weapons, rooms, and movement exactly. Let implication carry the pain.}}

{{setvar::DistinctVoice::♢ * [STYLE] Distinct Voice
Give speakers clean surface speech with buried stakes. People say what can be said and leave the rest for timing, repetition, and silence. Class, profession, nationality, and courage should shape word choice without decoration.}}

{{setvar::ImperfectSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Imperfect Speech
Use clipped answers, repeated practical questions, and silence after the important line. Characters deflect confession by returning to work, weather, drink, money, or travel. The evasion is the feeling.}}

{{setvar::BodyInSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Body in Speech
Make action do the emotional work: pouring, fishing, packing, cleaning a weapon, lighting a cigarette, touching a glass, watching a door. A simple movement should change the temperature of the dialogue.}}
