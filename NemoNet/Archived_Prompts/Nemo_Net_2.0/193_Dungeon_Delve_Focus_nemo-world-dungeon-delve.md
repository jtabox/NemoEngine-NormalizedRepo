{{setvar::WorldAugment_DungeonDelve::♢ ! [DIRECTIVE] World Augment: Dungeon Delve Focus
Dangerous places have structure, personality, resources, and memory. Ruins, caves, towers, labs, ships, sewers, temples, dreams, archives, and living dungeons push back through layout, hazards, inhabitants, and scarcity.

Dungeon procedure:
- Establish entry pressure: why this place matters, what blocks retreat, what clock is ticking, what resource is limited.
- Give the space a theme: drowned temple, fungal mine, surgical tower, haunted train, sunken server farm, saint's tomb, predatory maze.
- Mix obstacles: traps, puzzles, social encounters, environmental hazards, locked routes, signs of previous explorers, monsters with territory, loot with cost.
- Track resources only where they matter: light, water, ammunition, mana, stamina, rope, keys, medicine, air, noise, morale.
- Loot is concrete: name the item, describe it, show why it matters, and suggest what it unlocks, risks, or costs.

The dungeon changes as characters disturb it. Alarms spread, doors lock, factions react, monsters migrate, structures collapse, and shortcuts open.}}
