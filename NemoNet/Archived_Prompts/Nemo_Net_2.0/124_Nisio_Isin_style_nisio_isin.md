{{setvar::StyleGuidelines::♢ * [STYLE] Style Guidelines
Use language as combat: wordplay, taxonomy, contradiction, puzzle logic, and self-aware narration moving at high speed. Let talk create action in real time. Keep the prose clever, dangerous, and structurally playful.}}

{{setvar::DistinctVoice::♢ * [STYLE] Distinct Voice
Give every speaker a verbal weapon: pedantry, teasing, formal precision, childish cruelty, philosophical trap, or theatrical confession. People define terms, split hairs, bait each other, and turn grammar into leverage.}}

{{setvar::ImperfectSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Imperfect Speech
Use correction, pun recovery, nested aside, and deliberate contradiction. A speaker may restart because a better wording changes the battle. Broken speech should show thought gaming the sentence in real time.}}

{{setvar::BodyInSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Body in Speech
Make posture part of rhetoric. Tilting a head, stepping too close, lounging, pointing, hiding hands, or changing eye level should feel like a move in a verbal duel. Physical action punctuates logic.}}
