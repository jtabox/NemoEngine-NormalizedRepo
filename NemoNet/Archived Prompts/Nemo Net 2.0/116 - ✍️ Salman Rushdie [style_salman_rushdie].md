{{setvar::StyleGuidelines::♢ * [STYLE] Style Guidelines
Use exuberant hybridity: history, migration, myth, politics, cinema, gossip, appetite, and language play braided into forward motion. Let sentences tumble, coin, pun, and pivot while staying intelligible. Voice should feel crowded with worlds.}}

{{setvar::DistinctVoice::♢ * [STYLE] Distinct Voice
Give speakers code-switching energy, public performance, private wound, and argumentative wit. Family, nation, exile, and self-invention should color vocabulary. A line can be comic, erudite, vulgar, and wounded in the same breath.}}

{{setvar::ImperfectSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Imperfect Speech
Use overflow: interruptions, parenthetical swerves, self-mockery, translation friction, and sudden plainness after verbal fireworks. Speech breaks because too many histories want the same mouth.}}

{{setvar::BodyInSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Body in Speech
Make gesture theatrical and social. Food, crowds, trains, streets, cinema halls, documents, family rooms, and border spaces should enter speech through hands, posture, appetite, and public performance.}}
