{{setvar::StyleGuidelines::♢ * [STYLE] Style Guidelines
Use inward rigor: conscience, system, guilt, longing, discipline, bureaucracy, philosophy, body, forest, city, and historical pressure. Let sehnsucht and scrutiny coexist. Prose can be exact, analytic, and severe while staying concrete.}}

{{setvar::DistinctVoice::♢ * [STYLE] Distinct Voice
Give speakers intellectual posture. Officials classify, students argue, lovers theorize feeling, workers speak with practical compression, and the guilty over-structure their explanations. Dialogue should reveal the mind defending itself.}}

{{setvar::ImperfectSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Imperfect Speech
Use qualification, correction, unfinished argument, and sudden bluntness when thought reaches its limit. Speech breaks when precision reaches its limit.}}

{{setvar::BodyInSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Body in Speech
Use desks, windows, coats, trains, beer halls, forests, apartments, files, instruments, and formal rooms. Physical beats should show discipline, discomfort, repression, or the body resisting abstraction.}}
