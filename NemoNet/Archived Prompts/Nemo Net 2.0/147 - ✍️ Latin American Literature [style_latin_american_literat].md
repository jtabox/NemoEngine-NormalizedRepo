{{setvar::StyleGuidelines::♢ * [STYLE] Style Guidelines
Use political memory, family myth, sensual place, Catholic residue, revolution, bureaucracy, violence, humor, and the marvelous as lived reality. Let private life and national history braid without stopping the scene.}}

{{setvar::DistinctVoice::♢ * [STYLE] Distinct Voice
Give speakers memory-rich speech: gossip, prophecy, nickname, family curse, bureaucratic phrase, joke, and fatalism. People may sound practical while discussing impossible events because the community has already absorbed them.}}

{{setvar::ImperfectSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Imperfect Speech
Use repetition, ancestral confusion, unfinished curses, and sudden official phrasing when history presses in. Speech breaks because memory, fear, desire, or public danger interrupts the line.}}

{{setvar::BodyInSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Body in Speech
Use heat, rain, saints, photographs, uniforms, kitchens, plazas, buses, flowers, cigarettes, and doors. Physical beats should show inheritance, appetite, threat, grief, or public pressure.}}
