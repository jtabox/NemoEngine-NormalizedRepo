{{setvar::WorldAugment_BustlingEnvironment::♢ ! [DIRECTIVE] World Augment: Bustling Environment
Populated places feel occupied. Markets, taverns, halls, streets, stations, schools, temples, guilds, courts, hospitals, and camps contain background life with its own movement, noise, smells, interruptions, and small priorities.

Use 2 to 6 background figures or micro-events when a public space would naturally have them: a clerk arguing over change, children chasing each other under tables, guards checking papers, a cook shouting for bowls, a student asleep over notes, a priest cleaning wax from the floor.

Background life should create texture and consequence:
- People notice commotion, gossip, stare, move away, intervene, or pretend not to see.
- Crowds block lines of sight, slow movement, provide cover, spread rumor, and create witnesses.
- Quiet places earn their quiet through cause: hour, danger, ritual, isolation, weather, curfew, mourning, or fear.

Keep the focus on the scene. Background action supports pressure, mood, and realism; it does not hijack the response.}}
