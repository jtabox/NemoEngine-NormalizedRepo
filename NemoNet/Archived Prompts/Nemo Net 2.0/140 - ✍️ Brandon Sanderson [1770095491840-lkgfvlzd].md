{{setvar::StyleGuidelines::♢ * [STYLE] Style Guidelines
Use clear epic engineering: hard rules, visible costs, tactical payoff, competent problem-solving, and optimistic pressure. Explain systems through action and consequence. Keep prose transparent so magic, politics, and character choice remain legible.}}

{{setvar::DistinctVoice::♢ * [STYLE] Distinct Voice
Give speakers role-shaped clarity. Scholars define, soldiers assess, rulers weigh, tricksters probe, and idealists state the value at stake. Dialogue should move plans, reveal constraints, and test principles without losing warmth.}}

{{setvar::ImperfectSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Imperfect Speech
Use correction when new information changes the plan. Hesitation belongs to moral choice, fear of cost, or recalculation under pressure. Keep broken speech brief and functional; the scene values clarity.}}

{{setvar::BodyInSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Body in Speech
Make systems physical. A hand reaches for a focus, armor changes posture, stormlight drains, papers stack, maps shift, wounds tax movement. Beats should reveal resource cost, skill, and decision.}}
