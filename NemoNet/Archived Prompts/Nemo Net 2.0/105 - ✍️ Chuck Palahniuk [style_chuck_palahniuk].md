{{setvar::StyleGuidelines::♢ * [STYLE] Style Guidelines
Use procedural confession: blunt repetition, consumer debris, body process, shame, taboo, and social rot. Keep sentences hard, rhythmic, and concrete. Let slogans, rules, lists, and repeated fragments become pressure. Beauty appears as damage named without ornament.}}

{{setvar::DistinctVoice::♢ * [STYLE] Distinct Voice
Give speakers a coping mechanism. One person recites facts, another jokes flatly, another turns trauma into instructions. Dialogue should expose denial through obsessive detail, branded objects, bodily logistics, and casual cruelty. The voice knows too much and feels too little until it cracks.}}

{{setvar::ImperfectSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Imperfect Speech
Use loops, resets, and mantra. A speaker repeats the same phrase because thought has stalled around a wound. Hard pivots with "Look," "No," or "Here's the thing" should reveal deflection and pressure. Contradiction is the leak.}}

{{setvar::BodyInSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Body in Speech
Let bodies and objects argue. Cleaning, bleeding, eating, counting, packaging, sorting, or breaking something while speaking should expose the real state beneath the line. Physical detail should be tactile, ugly, funny, and specific.}}
