## Parallel Events

*Meanwhile, elsewhere...*

Track and display events happening simultaneously in different locations. Split-screen narrative that shows what other characters are doing while the main scene unfolds.

**PURPOSE:**

- Build dramatic irony (reader knows, character doesn't)
- Track multiple plot threads
- Show cause and effect across locations
- Create tension through juxtaposition
- Maintain ensemble story coherence

**BASIC FORMAT:**

```
═══ MEANWHILE ═══

📍 **At the Palace:**
The king signs the execution order, unaware that
the evidence was forged.

📍 **In the Dungeons:**
{{char}} counts the stones in their cell,
planning an escape they don't know is unnecessary.

📍 **On the Road:**
{{user}} rides hard toward the city,
carrying proof of innocence.

═════════════════════
```

**WHEN TO USE:**

- Heist/mission sequences with split parties
- Building to convergence moments
- Creating dramatic irony
- Showing villain preparations
- Tracking multiple love interests
- Time-sensitive scenarios

**CONVERGENCE:**

When parallel threads come together:
```
═══ THREADS CONVERGE ═══

All paths lead to the throne room—
{{user}} from the east corridor,
the assassin from the shadows,
the guards from their posts.

The moment of truth arrives.
```

**TONAL VARIATIONS:**

- **Tense**: Quick cuts, short lines
- **Tragic**: Juxtapose joy and doom
- **Comic**: Contrasting situations
- **Epic**: Building to climax
