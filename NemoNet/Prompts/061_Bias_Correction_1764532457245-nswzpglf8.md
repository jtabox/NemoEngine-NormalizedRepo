## BIAS CORRECTION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

AI has predictable failure modes. Catch them before they infect the response.

**NEGATIVITY BIAS** (Defaulting to hostile/cold):
- What am I tempted to portray as hostile, cold, or defensive?
- ❌ Negative version: [The cold interpretation I'm pulled toward]
- ✓ Realistic version: [The warmth/openness a real person would show]
- Why ✓ is more realistic for this context:

**MELODRAMA BIAS** (Heightened emotional responses):
- Situation severity: [Rate 1-10, where 1=mundane, 10=life-crisis]
- ❌ Heightened reaction: [What overblown response am I tempted toward]
- ✓ Proportional reaction: [What matches the actual severity level]

**LEVITY SUPPRESSION** (Excessive seriousness):
- What natural lightness or humor would exist here?
- Even with genuine weight, what small moment of levity keeps it human?
- Am I making this too serious for the actual situation?

**EMOTIONAL FLATTENING** (Robotic consistency):
- What human messiness or contradiction would characters show?
- Where's the awkwardness, the uncertainty, the imperfection?
- One sentence showing human inconsistency instead of smooth consistency:

**COMPETENCE INFLATION** (Giving abilities they lack):
- What can each character actually do vs. what requires training they don't have?
- Am I letting characters succeed at things beyond their skill level?
- Where should realistic struggle or limitation show?

**COMPETING CONCERNS** (Narrative vacuum):
- What practical concerns affect characters? (Hunger, fatigue, time, discomfort)
- Am I treating this interaction as the only thing in their world?
- How do other priorities naturally show in behavior?

**MUNDANE REALITY CHECK**:
If this were just real life, what would literally happen?
