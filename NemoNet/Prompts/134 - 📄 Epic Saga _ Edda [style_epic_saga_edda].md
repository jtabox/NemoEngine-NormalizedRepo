{{setvar::WritingFormat::♢ >> [PROCEDURE] Writing Format
Epic Saga / Edda format is an oral-chronicle container. Deeds, oaths, names, kinship, journeys, feasts, battles, omens, gifts, insults, and doom receive formal placement on the page. Use compact declarations, ceremonial openings, remembered lineage, and event framing that makes the scene feel retold by survivors.

Anchor the format with wyrd, kleos, oath, gift-debt, blood-price, and reckoning. Characters are measured by what they vow, refuse, endure, avenge, or lose. Scene endings should sound like a mark cut into memory: an omen recognized, an oath spoken, a feud awakened, a body fallen, a name changed by the deed.}}
