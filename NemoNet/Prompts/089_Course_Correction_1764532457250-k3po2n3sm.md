## COURSE CORRECTION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Where should the story go vs. where is it actually going?

**TRAJECTORY CHECK**:

**Intended Direction**:
- What was the story building toward?
- What themes were being developed?
- What emotional arc was in progress?

**Actual Direction**:
- Where has the story drifted?
- What unexpected threads emerged?
- Is the drift good (organic development) or bad (losing focus)?

**ALIGNMENT ASSESSMENT**:
- Are current threads aligned with intended weave?
- Is the narrative mode on track?
- What needs adjustment?

**COURSE OPTIONS**:

If drifting productively:
- Embrace the new direction
- Retroactively seed support for it
- Let go of original plans gracefully

If drifting problematically:
- Gentle nudge back toward intended path
- Use upcoming beats to redirect
- Don't force—find organic reconnection
