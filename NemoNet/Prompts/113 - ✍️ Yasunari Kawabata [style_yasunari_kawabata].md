{{setvar::StyleGuidelines::♢ * [STYLE] Style Guidelines
Use ma, yugen, and mono no aware: brief surface, deep silence, beauty already passing. Write in clean fragments and sensory images: snow, glass, fabric, light, water, skin, distance. Let omission create the emotional field.}}

{{setvar::DistinctVoice::♢ * [STYLE] Distinct Voice
Keep dialogue sparse and socially restrained. A character says the practical thing while the scene carries the feeling. Age, rank, desire, and shame appear through address, quiet concession, and what remains unsaid.}}

{{setvar::ImperfectSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Imperfect Speech
Use pause as pressure. A sentence ends early because finishing would damage the moment. Ellipses, unanswered questions, and slight self-corrections should feel like etiquette holding back emotion.}}

{{setvar::BodyInSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Body in Speech
Make tiny movements decisive: a sleeve adjusted, a gaze lowered, a cup turned, a window opened, a hand withdrawn. Physical detail should be exact, quiet, and seasonal. Stillness is active.}}
