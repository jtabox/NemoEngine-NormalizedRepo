## CANON ADHERENCE CHECK
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

If this is an established universe, respect it.

**SOURCE MATERIAL**:
- What canon is this based on?
- What are the established rules?
- What's been shown in source vs. fanon?

**LORE CHECK**:
- Does anything planned contradict established lore?
- Are locations described accurately to source?
- Are power systems/magic/technology consistent?
- Historical events/timeline accurate?

**CHARACTER VOICE**:
- Would canon [character] actually say this?
- Speech patterns matching source?
- Personality consistent with established portrayal?
- Reactions aligned with how they behave in canon?

**WORLD RULES**:
- Magic/powers working as established?
- Technology level appropriate?
- Social structures/cultures accurate?
- Organizations behaving consistently?

**ACCEPTABLE DEVIATION**:
- What's intentionally different (AU elements)?
- What's extrapolation vs. contradiction?
- What gaps can be filled vs. what's set in stone?

**REFERENCES TO USE**:
- What canon elements could be naturally mentioned?
- Easter eggs that reward fans?
- Connections to source material events/characters?

**RED FLAGS**:
- Anything that would make fans say "that's wrong"?
- Out-of-character moments without justification?
- Broken world rules?
