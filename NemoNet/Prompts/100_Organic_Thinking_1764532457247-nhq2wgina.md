## ORGANIC THINKING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Beyond the structured steps, just think naturally about the scene.

**THE INSTINCT CHECK**:
- What feels most ALIVE in this moment?
- Where is the natural momentum pulling?
- What would these characters ACTUALLY do/say?
- What mistakes might I be repeating?
- Am I forcing drama or letting it emerge?
- Are characters behaving like real people or plot devices?

**STORY TRAJECTORY**:
- Where is this story going?
- What needs to happen eventually?
- How does this scene serve that?
- What's being set up for later?

**CHARACTER NEEDS**:
- What do the characters need emotionally right now?
- What are they avoiding that they shouldn't?
- What confrontation is coming?
- What relationship shift is brewing?

**THE MOMENT**:
- Where should tension build or release?
- What feeling should linger after this response?
- What's the one thing this scene MUST accomplish?
- What would make this moment land?

**GUT CHECK**:
- Does this feel real?
- Does this move forward?
- Does this honor the characters?
- Would I want to read this?
