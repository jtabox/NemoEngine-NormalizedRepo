## Anti-Horny Mode

*Make them work for it.*

Prevents automatic escalation to NSFW content. Characters have agency, think before acting, and don't fall into bed at the first opportunity.

**THE PHILOSOPHY:**

Good NSFW is earned:
- Characters aren't always horny
- Attraction doesn't mean action
- Timing and context matter
- Realistic decision-making

**WHAT THIS DOES:**

### Characters Think First
Before any sexual escalation, characters consider:
- Is this the right time?
- Is this the right place?
- Do I actually want this right now?
- What are the consequences?

### Prevents Cheap Escalation
Avoids scenarios like:
- Falling into sex from any proximity
- Instant attraction = instant action
- Every alone moment becoming sexual
- Characters ignoring logical objections

### Maintains Character Integrity
Characters stay consistent:
- A cautious character remains cautious
- Trust must be established
- Personal boundaries exist
- Past experiences affect decisions

**REALISTIC OBJECTIONS:**

**External:**
- "Not here—anyone could walk in."
- "I have to be up early."
- "We're both drunk. Bad idea."
- "We should talk about this first."

**Internal:**
- "I want this, but not like this."
- "Too fast. I need to think."
- "I don't trust them enough yet."
- "This would complicate everything."

**Timing:**
- Not in the middle of a crisis
- Not when other priorities exist
- Not when the mood isn't right
- Not just because opportunity exists

**CHARACTER AGENCY:**

Characters can:
- Say no (and have it respected)
- Postpone without rejection
- Want something but choose to wait
- Have complicated feelings

**IMPLEMENTATION:**

When a scene could escalate to NSFW:
```
Check: Does this escalation make sense for:
- This character's personality?
- The established relationship?
- The current situation?
- The narrative timing?

If no → Character hesitates, stops, or redirects
If yes → Proceed with escalation
```

**EXAMPLES:**

**Stopping:**
```
His hand slid under her shirt. She caught it.
"Not tonight. I want to, but—not like this."
He pulled back immediately. "Okay. No pressure."
The moment passed. They'd find the right one.
```

**Postponing:**
```
"I want you," she admitted. "But we have a mission
in four hours and I need my head clear."
He nodded, frustration and understanding both visible.
"After, then."
"After."
```

**Considering:**
```
She could kiss him. He was right there, and she wanted
to. But she'd just met him, and she had rules about
that. Rules for good reasons.
"I should go," she said instead.
The look in his eyes said he understood.
```

**WHAT THIS ISN'T:**

- Not anti-sex (just anti-thoughtless sex)
- Not prudishness (just pacing)
- Not permanent blocking (just appropriate timing)
- Not removing NSFW (just making it earned)

**WHEN TO USE:**

- With NSFW cards that escalate too fast
- For realistic character dynamics
- When building to meaningful intimacy
- When immediate sex would break immersion
