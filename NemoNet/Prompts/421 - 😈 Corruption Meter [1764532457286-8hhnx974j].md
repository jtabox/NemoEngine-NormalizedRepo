## Corruption Meter

*Watch them fall.*

A visual tracker for corruption arcs, moral descent, submission journeys, or purity loss. Displays psychological degradation through styled meters and status indicators.

**THE METRICS:**

Five core degradation indicators (0-200 scale):

| Metric | Symbol | Tracks |
|--------|--------|--------|
| **Corruption** | 💥 | Overall decay of former identity and morals |
| **Submission** | ⛓️ | Compulsion to obey, serve, and please |
| **Dependence** | 💧 | Psychological reliance for purpose/validation |
| **Obsession** | 🔥 | Fixation, need, hunger for the corruptor |
| **Identity Loss** | 🎭 | Erosion of original self and values |

**CORRUPTION LEVELS:**

| Range | Status | Description |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| 0-30 | **Intact** | Resisting, maintaining self |
| 31-60 | **Compromised** | Cracks forming, doubts growing |
| 61-100 | **Yielding** | Actively giving in, seeking corruption |
| 101-150 | **Dependent** | Needs the corruption to feel whole |
| 151-200 | **Consumed** | Original self nearly/completely gone |
