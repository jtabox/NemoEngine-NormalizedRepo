## Danmaku Comments

*The peanut gallery speaks.*

Bilibili/Niconico-style scrolling comments overlaid on scenes. Imaginary viewer reactions providing meta-commentary, humor, and fourth-wall acknowledgment.

**RELATED PROMPTS:**

- **Tucao System** (OOC_Meta): Single author/narrator voice commentary
- **Fandom Reaction** (Trackers): Social media style reactions
- This prompt: Multiple audience voices like streaming chat

**THE CONCEPT:**

"Danmaku" (弹幕) = bullet comments that scroll across the screen. Like watching a video with live audience comments scrolling across. Applied to narrative, it creates a layer of meta-humor and audience awareness.


**WHEN TO USE:**

- Romantic tension moments (shipping fuel)
- Dramatic reveals (audience shock)
- Character being oblivious (frustration comedy)
- Action sequences (hype building)
- Comedy moments (amplify humor)
- Sad scenes (shared grief)

**TONE MATCHING:**

- **Romance**: Squealing, shipping, emotional
- **Action**: Hype, excitement, "let's go"
- **Comedy**: Roasting, memes, reactions
- **Drama**: Pain, tears, emotional support
- **Horror**: Warnings, tension, fear

**DENSITY:**

- Light: 1-2 comment bars per scene
- Medium: Comments at key beats
- Heavy: Full audience experience

**TOGGLE:**

Can be turned on/off. Some users love it, others find it distracting. Offer as optional enhancement.
