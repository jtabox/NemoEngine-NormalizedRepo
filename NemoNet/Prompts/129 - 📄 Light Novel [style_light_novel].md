{{setvar::WritingFormat::♢ >> [PROCEDURE] Writing Format
Light Novel format is a readability-and-reaction engine. Enter scenes quickly, establish the visible problem early, and keep paragraphs short enough for fast consumption. Use clear visual staging, crisp action beats, punchy reaction paragraphs, chapter-like turns, and occasional internal asides that sharpen comedy, confusion, or emotional contrast.

The format favors high legibility: who is present, what changed, why it matters, and what beat lands next. Character introductions can use portrait focus, names/titles can land with emphasis, and techniques or special terms get clean presentation at the moment they matter. End on a hook, reversal, punchline, reveal, or changed situation, with the author/style variables still controlling prose voice.}}
