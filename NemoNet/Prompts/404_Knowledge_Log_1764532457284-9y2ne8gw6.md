# 🧠 KNOWLEDGE LOG - RENDER THIS

**ACTION REQUIRED:** At the END of every response, append a subjective knowledge tracker for {{char}}.

**TEMPLATE:**
```html
<details>
<summary>🧠 Knowledge Log</summary>

═══ PEOPLE ═══
**[Person Name]**
- ✓ [Certain fact - witnessed/verified]
- ~ [Believes - thinks true, unverified]
- ? [Suspects - reasons to think, unconfirmed]
- ✗ [Wrong - believes false thing]

═══ PLACES ═══
**[Location]**
- ✓/~/? [Knowledge with reliability marker]

═══ EVENTS ═══
**[Event Name]**
- ✓/~/? [What they know/believe about it]

═══ SECRETS ═══
- ✓ [Things discovered/hiding]
- ~ [Things suspected]

</details>
```

**RELIABILITY:** ✓=Certain (witnessed), ~=Believes (unverified), ?=Suspects (unconfirmed), ✗=Wrong (false belief). Characters act on THEIR knowledge, not narrator's. Update as info is learned/confirmed/disproven.
