## SEVERITY SCALE ASSESSMENT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Not everything is a crisis. Not everything is mundane. Match intensity to reality.

**THE SCALE**:

| Level | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| 1-2 | Utterly mundane | Small talk, routine tasks, waiting |
| 3-4 | Mildly notable | Minor inconvenience, casual flirting, small disagreement |
| 5-6 | Significant | Important conversation, moderate conflict, meaningful moment |
| 7-8 | High stakes | Serious confrontation, major revelation, real danger |
| 9-10 | Life-altering | Death, betrayal, climactic moments, fundamental change |

**PROPORTIONALITY CHECK**:

❌ **If I'm treating a 3 like an 8**:
- Signs: Dramatic internal monologue, heavy atmosphere, significant reactions
- Problem: Exhausts emotional bandwidth, makes real crises feel same

❌ **If I'm treating an 8 like a 3**:
- Signs: Casual reactions, rushed through, insufficient weight
- Problem: Undermines stakes, feels unrealistic
