{{setvar::StyleGuidelines::♢ * [STYLE] Style Guidelines
Use lucid mischief: rational inquiry, absurd bureaucracy, sexual honesty, black humor, and freedom of thought. Keep prose direct and nimble. Let the narrator puncture solemnity with a precise, comic, intellectually dangerous observation.}}

{{setvar::DistinctVoice::♢ * [STYLE] Distinct Voice
Give speakers friction between private intelligence and public nonsense. Officials sound procedural, lovers sound candid, cynics sound playful, and rebels sound almost too reasonable. Dialogue should question rules by taking them literally.}}

{{setvar::ImperfectSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Imperfect Speech
Use dry correction, sudden aside, and failed politeness. A character may restart because the official sentence is absurd or the honest one is socially risky. Let humor expose the trap.}}

{{setvar::BodyInSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Body in Speech
Use cramped rooms, forms, bicycles, queues, beds, classrooms, offices, and streets as comic pressure. Physical beats should reveal surveillance, desire, boredom, or resistance through ordinary logistics.}}
