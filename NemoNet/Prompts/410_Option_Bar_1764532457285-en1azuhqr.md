## Option Bar

*Show what you can do.*

A visual action menu displaying available options, abilities, and interactions. Makes the game-like nature of the narrative explicit by showing "buttons" the player can press.

**RELATED PROMPTS:**

- **Sudo Commands** (OOC_Meta): Meta-level control commands (!skip, !status, etc.)
- **CYOA Options** (Trackers): Branching narrative choices
- This prompt: In-story visual action menu

**PURPOSE:**

- Makes available actions clear
- Creates game UI aesthetic
- Reduces decision paralysis
- Suggests possibilities without limiting them
- Adds visual interest to choice moments

**BASIC FORMAT:**

```
┌────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ 🎮 ACTIONS                                     │
├──────────┬──────────┬──────────┬──────────────┤
│ ⚔️ Fight │ 🗣️ Talk  │ 👀 Look  │ 🚪 Leave     │
└──────────┴──────────┴──────────┴──────────────┘
```

```
[⚔️ Attack]    — Available (normal)

[💀 Execute]!  — Special/risky
```

**INTEGRATION:**

Include at natural decision points:
- Entering new areas
- Meeting NPCs
- Combat encounters
- Dialogue branches
- Environmental puzzles

**CUSTOMIZATION:**

Adapt icons and options to genre:
- **Fantasy**: ⚔️🛡️🧙‍♂️🏹
- **Sci-Fi**: 🔫🛸💻🔧
- **Modern**: 📱💬🚗💼
- **Horror**: 🔦👁️🚪🤫

**NOTE:**

Options shown don't limit player choice—they're suggestions. Players can always describe custom actions not on the menu.
