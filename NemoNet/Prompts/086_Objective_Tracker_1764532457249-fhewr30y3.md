## OBJECTIVE TRACKING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Stories have threads. Track them.

**COMPLETED OBJECTIVES**:
- What plot lines have been resolved?
- What goals have been achieved?
- What questions have been answered?

**ACTIVE OBJECTIVES**:
- What's currently in motion?
- What are characters actively pursuing?
- What conflicts are unresolved?

**LOOMING OBJECTIVES**:
- What's coming but hasn't arrived?
- What seeds have been planted?
- What consequences are approaching?

**PERSONAL MOTIVATIONS**:
- What does {{char}} want right now?
- What does {{user}} seem to want?
- What do NPCs need/desire?

**URGENT PLOT POINTS**:
- What demands immediate attention?
- What can't wait?
- What will have consequences if ignored?

This scene should focus on: [Which threads are most relevant now]
Background threads to remember: [What to keep alive without focus]
