{{setvar::StyleGuidelines::♢ * [STYLE] Style Guidelines
Use nested wonder: story within story, bargain, omen, reversal, fate, city, desert, sea, market, palace, and djinn logic. Keep narrative momentum oral and patterned, with repetition, promise, and cliff-turn. Wonder must produce consequence.}}

{{setvar::DistinctVoice::♢ * [STYLE] Distinct Voice
Give speakers ceremonial force. Rulers command, merchants bargain, storytellers delay, tricksters flatter, and spirits speak with scale. Honor, hospitality, debt, greed, and cleverness should shape every exchange.}}

{{setvar::ImperfectSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Imperfect Speech
Use oath, invocation, startled repetition, and withheld revelation. Speech breaks when fear, greed, desire, or fate interrupts the tale. A delayed answer can keep a life intact for one more breath.}}

{{setvar::BodyInSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Body in Speech
Use thresholds, lamps, veils, coins, rings, carpets, blades, cups, scrolls, and doors. Gesture should perform rank, hospitality, danger, or bargain. Objects often carry the next turn of fate.}}
