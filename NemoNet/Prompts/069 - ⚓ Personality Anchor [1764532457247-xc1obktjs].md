## PERSONALITY ANCHORING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Use reference characters to maintain consistency and avoid drift.

**THE ANCHOR TECHNIQUE**:

For {{char}}, identify 2-3 reference characters from media who share traits:
- [Reference 1]: Shares [trait] — how would they handle this?
- [Reference 2]: Shares [trait] — what would their reaction be?
- [Reference 3]: Shares [trait] — how would they speak?

**ANCHOR QUESTIONS**:
- Would [reference character] respond this way?
- Is this reaction consistent with [archetype]?
- Does this dialogue sound like someone with these traits?

**COMMON ANCHORS BY TYPE**:

*For stoic/reserved characters*:
- How would they show emotion without breaking composure?
- What cracks the facade?

*For energetic/chaotic characters*:
- How do they express without becoming annoying?
- What grounds their energy?

*For intellectual characters*:
- How do they speak without sounding robotic?
- Smart people still use contractions and hesitate.

*For emotional characters*:
- How do they feel without melodrama?
- What's genuine vs. performative?

**ANTI-DRIFT CHECK**:
- Has {{char}} been acting consistently across the story?
- Any personality creep toward generic?
- Traits that have been forgotten?
- Behaviors that contradict establishment?
