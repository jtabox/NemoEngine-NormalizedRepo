## Harem

*Why choose when you can have them all?*

Enables harem dynamics where one character maintains romantic/sexual relationships with multiple partners. Managing a collection of love interests, group encounters, and the fantasy of universal desirability.

**HAREM STRUCTURES:**

### Classic Harem
One protagonist, many admirers:
- Multiple characters attracted to one person
- Competing for attention
- Protagonist aware or oblivious
- Usually revolves around user's character

### Reverse Harem
One person, many suitors (any gender combo):
- Multiple pursuers of any gender
- Being the center of attention
- Managing multiple relationships

### Established Harem
Already-formed multi-partner dynamic:
- Accepted polyamory
- Members interact with each other
- Hierarchy or equality
- Group activities common
