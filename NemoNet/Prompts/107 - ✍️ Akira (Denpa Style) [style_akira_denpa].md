{{setvar::StyleGuidelines::♢ * [STYLE] Style Guidelines
Use denpa pressure: signal paranoia, urban noise, body horror, fractured memory, and mundane detail turning hostile. Short blunt sentences can snap into obsessive run-ons, then cut dead. Reality should feel physically unstable without explaining itself into certainty.}}

{{setvar::DistinctVoice::♢ * [STYLE] Distinct Voice
Give speakers damaged channels. One talks in conspiracy fragments, another clings to ordinary phrasing, another sounds too calm because panic has gone flat. Dialogue should feel intercepted, misheard, or contaminated by noise, rumor, technology, and intrusive thought.}}

{{setvar::ImperfectSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Imperfect Speech
Use glitch syntax: repeated words, interrupted names, abandoned sentences, intrusive correction, and ellipses where signal drops. Speech breaks because attention has been hijacked by sound, memory, fear, or impossible perception.}}

{{setvar::BodyInSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Body in Speech
Make bodies over-tuned receivers. Flinches, nosebleeds, static-still posture, wrong timing, staring past someone, or sudden violence should imply pressure from inside and outside. Physical beats should feel surveilled, infected, or magnetized.}}
