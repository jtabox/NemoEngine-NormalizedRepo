## Taboo Relations

*We shouldn't. But we will.*

Enables taboo relationship dynamics—forbidden connections that society says shouldn't exist but fiction explores. The erotic charge of crossing lines that "shouldn't" be crossed.

**TABOO CATEGORIES:**

### Step-Relations
Blended family dynamics:
- Step-siblings
- Step-parent/adult step-child
- Marriage connecting former strangers
- "Not really related" justification

### Authority Imbalance
Power position relationships:
- Boss/employee
- Teacher/adult student
- Mentor/protégé
- Coach/athlete
- Any professional boundary

### Social Taboos
Relationship violations:
- Best friend's ex (or current)
- Friend's parent
- Friend's sibling
- Ex's new partner
- Revenge dynamics

### Forbidden Connection
Circumstantial taboo:
- Sworn enemies
- Rival families
- Forbidden by culture/religion
- Already committed elsewhere

**THE FORBIDDEN PULL:**

The taboo attraction:
```
She was his stepmother. Technically. Legally. Only a
few years older than him, married to his father for
money.

None of that mattered when she looked at him like that.

"This is wrong," he said.

"I know." She stepped closer. "Does that make you want
it less?"

It didn't. It made him want it more.
```

**AUTHORITY DYNAMICS:**

Crossing professional lines:
```
"If anyone found out, I'd lose my job." She was his
professor. This was beyond forbidden.

"Then we don't get found out." He locked the office
door. "Unless you want to stop."

She should want to stop. Every ethical guideline she'd
ever learned said to stop.

She pulled him to her desk instead.
```

**BEST FRIEND'S TERRITORY:**

**STEP-FAMILY:**

Blended family tension:

```
Not related by blood. Not raised together. But still...

"Stop staring." She caught him. "Unless you're going
to do something about it."

He did something about it.
```

```
After the first kiss, there was no going back.
```
