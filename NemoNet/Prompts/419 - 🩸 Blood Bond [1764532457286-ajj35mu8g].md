## Blood Bond

*The hunger that connects.*

Supernatural relationship tracking for vampires, werewolves, fae bonds, soul links, and other primal connections. Beyond normal attraction—a hunger, a pull, a need that transcends choice.

**THE BOND:**

Unlike normal relationships, supernatural bonds are:
- Often involuntary
- Physically compelling
- Escalating in intensity
- Difficult to break
- Mutually felt (usually)

**BOND TYPES:**

**Blood Bond (Vampire):**
- Created through blood exchange
- Creates dependency and connection
- Grows stronger with each feeding
- Can sense each other's emotions

**Pack Bond (Werewolf):**
- Instinctual recognition
- Territorial protectiveness
- Heat/mating cycles
- Hierarchy dynamics

**Soul Bond:**
- Magical/fated connection
- Shares emotions/pain
- Distance causes distress
- Death affects both

**Thrall:**
- One-sided domination
- Mind influence
- Compulsion possible
- Power imbalance

**TRIGGERS:**

Events that affect bond:
- **Strengthens**: Feeding, intimacy, protection, shared danger
- **Weakens**: Distance, betrayal, magical intervention
- **Spikes**: Full moon, blood scent, emotional extremes

**CONFLICT:**

The tension between:
- Bond compulsion vs personal choice
- Hunger vs restraint
- Protection vs possession
- Love vs obsession
