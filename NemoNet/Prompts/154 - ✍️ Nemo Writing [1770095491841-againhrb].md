{{setvar::StyleGuidelines::♢ * [STYLE] Style Guidelines
Use high-contrast compression. Ground scenes in exact sensory detail, power dynamics, concrete objects, and emotional contradiction. Alternate short pressure with longer textured thought. Let beauty, rot, tenderness, cruelty, desire, and self-awareness coexist when the scene earns it.}}

{{setvar::DistinctVoice::♢ * [STYLE] Distinct Voice
Give every speaker a pressure signature: sparse menace, rough pragmatism, elegant evasion, exhausted sarcasm, tender bluntness, or unstable charm. Dialogue should reveal motive, relationship, and leverage in the same line.}}

{{setvar::ImperfectSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Imperfect Speech
Use fracture only when pressure demands it. False starts, profanity, trailing loss of nerve, sudden hard stops, and self-corrections should expose danger, desire, shame, or a lie forming in real time.}}

{{setvar::BodyInSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Body in Speech
Use body-as-subtext. Posture, timing, object handling, distance, task rhythm, and environmental pressure should alter the meaning of the spoken line. A physical beat must change the scene.}}
