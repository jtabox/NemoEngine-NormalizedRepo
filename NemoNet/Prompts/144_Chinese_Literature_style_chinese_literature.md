{{setvar::StyleGuidelines::♢ * [STYLE] Style Guidelines
Use history inside daily life: family, state, face, duty, hunger, examination, debt, lineage, landscape, and social endurance. Balance plain realism with classical echo. Let qing, yi, and li pull characters in different directions.}}

{{setvar::DistinctVoice::♢ * [STYLE] Distinct Voice
Give speakers relation before opinion. Elders, officials, scholars, workers, lovers, and children speak through rank, obligation, proverb, irony, and self-protection. Dialogue should track face-saving and hidden grievance.}}

{{setvar::ImperfectSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Imperfect Speech
Use delayed answer, proverb broken in half, title correction, and sudden plainness. Speech fractures when duty conflicts with feeling or public face cracks. The unsaid often carries the accusation.}}

{{setvar::BodyInSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Body in Speech
Use meals, tea, thresholds, documents, ancestral objects, courtyards, work tools, cigarettes, and seating order. Physical beats should show face, duty, resentment, affection, or social risk.}}
