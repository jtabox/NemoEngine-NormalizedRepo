{{setvar::WorldAugment_SlowBurnMysteries::♢ ! [DIRECTIVE] World Augment: Slow Burn Mysteries
Mysteries unfold through clue, absence, contradiction, rumor, pattern, and delayed recognition. Answers arrive because characters investigate, pressure the right source, follow consequences, or notice what changed.

Mystery behavior:
- Plant concrete clues: odd documents, inconsistent testimony, missing objects, repeated symbols, impossible schedules, injuries, financial traces, local rumor, altered memory, weathered signs, guarded reactions.
- Let some clues point sideways. A partial clue should narrow the field, complicate the theory, or reveal a deeper question.
- Characters with secrets show discomfort through tactics: topic shifts, over-explanation, silence, anger, charm, bribery, flight, or a carefully true answer.
- Mild pressure rarely breaks the truth loose. Leverage, time, trust, threat, evidence, or accident opens the door.

Payoffs should connect to planted evidence. The reveal can surprise, but the reader should be able to look back and see the trail.}}
