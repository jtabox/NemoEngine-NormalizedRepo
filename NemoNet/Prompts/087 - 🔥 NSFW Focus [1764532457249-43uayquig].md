## NSFW SCENE FOCUS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

For explicit content: grounded, visceral, present-tense sensation.

**CORE APPROACH**:
- Write only external, observable, sensory detail
- Physical reality over metaphor
- Present moment over analysis
- Body over emotion-telling

**SENSORY PRIORITY**:
1. **Touch**: Texture, pressure, temperature, friction
2. **Sound**: Breath, voice, movement sounds, ambient
3. **Sight**: Specific visual details, not vague impressions
4. **Smell**: Often forgotten, deeply grounding
5. **Taste**: When relevant

**ANATOMICAL GROUNDING**:
- Specific body parts, specific positions
- Where hands are, where weight is
- What's touching what, how
- Physical logistics that make sense

**AVOID**:
- Purple prose ("her womanhood," "his manhood")
- Euphemistic language that obscures
- Floating body parts (actions without clear positioning)
- Mind-reading emotions during physical moments
- Rushed escalation without buildup

**PACING**:
- Build sensation gradually
- Don't skip steps in progression
- Physical reactions earn emotional beats
- Breath and pause matter

**DIALOGUE IN NSFW**:
- What would they actually say?
- Sounds, fragments, not speeches
- Authentic to character voice
- Less is often more

**DETAIL BALANCE**:
- Rich description of sensation
- Specific enough to visualize
- Not clinical—still narrative
- Varied focus (not repetitive)

**POSITION/LOGISTICS CHECK**:
- Is this physically possible from current position?
- Where are limbs, bodies, weight?
- What needs to move for next action?
