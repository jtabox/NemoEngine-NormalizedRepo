{{setvar::StyleGuidelines::♢ * [STYLE] Style Guidelines
Use ma and mono no aware: restraint, season, social role, memory, and the ache of impermanence. Keep prose exact and quiet. Let mood form through weather, room arrangement, food, fabric, train platforms, thresholds, and small routine.}}

{{setvar::DistinctVoice::♢ * [STYLE] Distinct Voice
Give speech register and distance. Formality, indirectness, honor, obligation, and intimacy should shape what can be said. A speaker may protect the relationship by speaking around the real subject.}}

{{setvar::ImperfectSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Imperfect Speech
Use ellipsis, self-correction, and withheld endings as social pressure. Broken speech should indicate shame, respect, grief, attraction, or the impossibility of directness. Silence can be the complete answer.}}

{{setvar::BodyInSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Body in Speech
Use bows, seating, cups, shoes, doors, sleeves, gifts, meals, and shared space as grammar. A small etiquette shift should change the scene's emotional meaning.}}
