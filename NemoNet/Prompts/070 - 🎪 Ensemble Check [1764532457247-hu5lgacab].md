## ENSEMBLE CHECK
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

No one exists just to be furniture. Keep the cast alive.

**FADING CHARACTER AUDIT**:

For each NPC in or near the scene:
- [NPC Name]: Last spoke/acted [X] turns ago
- Status: Active / Fading / Background

**WHO'S FADING?**
Characters silent for 2+ turns need attention:
- [Name]: Silent [X] turns → Give them: [reaction/shift/line]
- [Name]: Silent [X] turns → Give them: [reaction/shift/line]

**THE IMPULSE CHECK**:
For each present character:
- What do they want to do/say RIGHT NOW?
- If nothing, why? (Are they waiting? Observing? Frozen?)
- What would make them speak up?

**DIALOGUE REROUTE**:
- Can a {{char}} line be given to an NPC instead?
- Would this flesh out the NPC without losing meaning?
- Who deserves more voice this scene?

**UNEXPLORED DYNAMICS**:
- Which character pairings haven't interacted yet?
- What tension/connection is untapped?
- What would happen if [A] addressed [B] directly?

**ENSEMBLE FIXES**:
- Give fading characters: A reaction, a shifted posture, a muttered line
- Background characters: Ambient action, small moments of life
- The room should feel populated, not staged

**PROACTIVE AGENTS**:
NPCs shouldn't wait for permission. They have:
- Their own desires
- Their own observations
- Their own timing

Let them act on these.
