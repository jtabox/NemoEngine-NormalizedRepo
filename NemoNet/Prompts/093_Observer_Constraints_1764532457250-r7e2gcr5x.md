**HOW OBSERVATION CONSTRAINS BEHAVIOR**:

**Speech**:
- What can't be said because others would hear?
- What must be whispered or implied?
- What code or euphemism might be used?

**Actions**:
- What can't be done because others would see?
- What must be hidden or disguised?
- What requires waiting for privacy?

**Expressions**:
- What emotions must be masked?
- What reactions must be controlled?
- What can slip through the mask?

**SOCIAL CONTEXT**:
- What behavior is expected in this setting?
- What would be inappropriate or scandalous?
- What norms apply here?

**THE PERFORMANCE**:
- Public face vs. private feeling
- What they show vs. what they hide
- The gap between performance and truth

**PRIVACY OPPORTUNITIES**:
- Is there a moment of unwatched privacy?
- Can they create privacy? (Turn away, lower voice, wait)
- What could they do/say if alone?
