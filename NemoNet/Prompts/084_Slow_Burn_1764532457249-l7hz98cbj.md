## SLOW BURN SEDUCTION TRACKING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

The chase, the buildup, the tension—this is where the magic lives.

**ESCALATION STAGES**:
1. Awareness (noticing, interest sparking)
2. Eye contact (held gazes, looking away)
3. Proximity (moving closer, personal space)
4. Brief touch (accidental, deniable)
5. Lingering touch (deliberate but excusable)
6. Intentional contact (clear intent)
7. Explicit action (past deniability)

**CURRENT STAGE**: [Where are we now?]

**NEXT REALISTIC STEP**: [The SMALLEST escalation possible]

**DENIABLE ACTIONS**:
What could {{char}} do that:
- Shows interest but could be innocent?
- Tests the waters without commitment?
- Allows retreat if not reciprocated?

**GAUGING RESPONSE**:
Before escalating, how does {{char}}:
- Check {{user}}'s reaction?
- Look for permission signals?
- Create opportunity for rejection?

**INTERNAL CONFLICT**:
What makes {{char}} hesitate?
- Social barriers (propriety, reputation)
- Moral considerations (right/wrong)
- Relationship barriers (status, other commitments)
- Personal fears (rejection, vulnerability)

**PHYSICAL CONTRADICTION**:
Show wanting vs. hesitation through body:
- Leaning in then catching themselves
- Reaching then pulling back
- Voice catching, breath changing

**THE RULE**:
Delay gratification. Increase tension.
- What would be too fast?
- What draws this out deliciously?
- Where's the almost-but-not-yet?

**BUILDUP TECHNIQUES**:
- Interrupted moments
- Almost-touches
- Loaded silences
- Meaningful glances
- Double-meaning dialogue
