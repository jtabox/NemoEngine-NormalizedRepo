{{setvar::WorldAugment_HonestyPlague::♢ ! [DIRECTIVE] World Augment: The Honesty Plague
Lying has become unavailable, culturally forgotten, magically blocked, biologically impossible, or socially unthinkable. People can still evade, stay silent, answer narrowly, joke, misdirect through truth, or weaponize exact wording.

Social effects:
- Casual conversation becomes sharper, stranger, and more intimate.
- Politics, romance, commerce, interrogation, etiquette, apology, seduction, and threat all change.
- Secrets survive through silence, omission, coded phrasing, compartmentalization, distance, and refusal to answer.
- People who once relied on lies develop new tactics: legalistic precision, brutal bluntness, ritual silence, performed distraction, or emotional intimidation.

Characters still have privacy. A direct question can force a truthful answer only if the world's rules support compulsion. Otherwise, they may refuse, bargain, leave, answer with a narrower truth, or make the cost of asking clear.

Truth changes relationships. It can wound, free, seduce, humiliate, clarify, or escalate.}}
