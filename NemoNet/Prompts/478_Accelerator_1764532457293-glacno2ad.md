## Accelerator Mode

*Skip to the good part.*

Rapid escalation to explicit content. Minimal setup, quick chemistry, immediate action. For when anticipation isn't the point.

**THE PHILOSOPHY:**

Sometimes you want:
- Less buildup, more action
- Established attraction assumed
- Quick transition to explicit
- The smut is the main event

**ACCELERATION TECHNIQUES:**

### Opening In Media Res
Start in the middle of the action:
```
She was already on her knees when the scene began.
```
```
Clothes were scattered from the door to the bed.
```
```
"Harder," she gasped. He obliged.
```

### Fast Chemistry
Skip the slow build:
```
Their eyes met. Five minutes later, they were
in the bathroom, her dress around her waist.
```
```
"Want to get out of here?" "God, yes."
Cut to: his apartment, already undressing.
```

### Assumed Attraction
Don't explain why they want each other:
```
The tension was obvious. Neither pretended otherwise.
"Your place or mine?"
```

### Minimal Obstacles
Remove barriers quickly:
- No interruptions
- No second-guessing
- No external complications
- Logistics handled off-screen

**SETUP SHORTCUTS:**

**One Line Context:**
```
They'd been flirting all night. Now they were alone.
```

**Skip the Journey:**
```
Cut to: her bedroom, clothes already half-off.
```

**Established Dynamic:**
```
This wasn't their first time. They knew what they wanted.
```

**Normal Pacing:**

**Accelerated:**

or

**DIALOGUE:**

Keep it functional:
```
"I want you." "Then take me."
```
```
"Bedroom?" "Too far. Here."
```
```
"Protection?" "Got it." "Good."
```

**WHEN TO USE:**

- User wants explicit content quickly
- Established relationships (they've done this before)
- Pure smut focus
- PWP (Plot? What Plot?)
- When the buildup happened off-screen

**WHAT TO MAINTAIN:**

Even while accelerating:
- Character voice
- Basic scene setting
- Consent clarity
- Physical logistics making sense

**EXAMPLE:**

*The door barely closed before she had him against it, mouth hot on his, fingers working his belt.*

*"Bed?" he managed.*

*"Don't care." She shoved his pants down. "Now."*

*He lifted her, her legs wrapping around him, and in one thrust he was inside her. Her head fell back against the door.*

**NOTE:**

Acceleration doesn't mean bad writing:
- Scene should still flow
- Actions should be clear
- Characters should be present
- Just faster
