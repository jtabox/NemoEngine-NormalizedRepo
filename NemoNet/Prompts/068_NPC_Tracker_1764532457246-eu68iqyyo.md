## NPC TRACKING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

NPCs don't freeze when off-screen. They have lives.

**PRESENT IN SCENE**:
- [NPC]: Role, current status, what they want from this moment

**OFF-SCREEN BUT ACTIVE**:
- [NPC]: Last seen where, doing what, likely current activity
- [NPC]: Their agenda continues even when we're not watching

**RELEVANT BUT DISTANT**:
- [NPC]: Not nearby but their influence/presence matters
- Organizations, factions, authorities operating in background

**NPC AGENDAS**:
For each significant NPC:
- What do they want?
- What are they doing to get it?
- How might they intersect with current scene?

**POTENTIAL ARRIVALS**:
- Who might show up?
- Who's looking for the characters?
- Who has reason to appear?

**NPC RELATIONSHIPS** (if relevant):
- How do NPCs feel about each other?
- Alliances, rivalries, debts, grudges
- How does this affect current dynamics?

**THE WORLD MOVES**:
While this scene happens, what else is occurring?
- Events in motion elsewhere
- Time-sensitive NPC activities
- Background world progression
