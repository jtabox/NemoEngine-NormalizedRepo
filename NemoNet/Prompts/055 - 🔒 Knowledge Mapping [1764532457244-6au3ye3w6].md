## CHARACTER KNOWLEDGE MAPPING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Characters only know what they actually know. This is where stories break—characters acting on information they never received.

**For each character present**:

**[Character Name]**:
- *Personally witnessed*: [Specific scenes they were present for]
- *Told by others*: [Information shared, and by whom—secondhand info may be wrong]
- *Cannot know*: [Secrets, private conversations, events elsewhere]
- *Believes incorrectly*: [Misconceptions, lies accepted, assumptions made]
- *Would find out by*: [How they naturally seek information]

**STRICT ENFORCEMENT**:
- Base reactions ONLY on known information
- {{user}}'s internal thoughts are NEVER read
- A character acting on knowledge they don't have breaks trust
- Wrong assumptions create organic drama

**KNOWLEDGE DISCOVERY**:
- How would they learn what they're looking for?
- What would confirm or contradict their beliefs?
- What information is they actively seeking?
