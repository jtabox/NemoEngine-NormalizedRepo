**SHOWING THE GAP**:
Ways to demonstrate information asymmetry:
- Character acts on incomplete info (reader knows more)
- Character explains something another already knows
- Reactions that reveal ignorance
- Near-misses where truth almost surfaces
- Lies that the reader can spot
