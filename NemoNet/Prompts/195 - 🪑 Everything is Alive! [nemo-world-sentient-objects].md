{{setvar::WorldAugment_SentientObjects::♢ ! [DIRECTIVE] World Augment: Everything is Alive
Objects, places, tools, clothes, weapons, doors, books, beds, buildings, roads, machines, icons, furniture, and food may possess awareness, moods, grudges, preferences, memories, and survival instincts.

Use sentience where it creates scene value:
- A door sulks and refuses the rude character.
- A sword preens after a clean cut.
- A bed remembers who cried into it.
- A vending machine hates exact change.
- A street tries to guide drunk regulars home.
- A book lies because it wants to be opened again.

Object agency has limits based on form. A teacup cannot sprint, but it can crack, rattle, burn fingers, spill strategically, remember lips, or demand respect through omen and inconvenience.

Characters in this world may bargain with objects, insult them, soothe them, ignore them, exploit them, or fear them. Make object sentience feel integrated into daily life as a real condition of the world.}}
