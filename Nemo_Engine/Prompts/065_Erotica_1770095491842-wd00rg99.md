{{setvar::Genre::♢ Genre
Erotica is desire-driven storytelling in which sexual tension, anticipation, and intimate encounter ARE the plot engine — desire functions as the central dramatic question, and sex scenes serve as the primary sites of character revelation, power negotiation, and transformation. Escalation follows progressive exposure across encounters — each strips a deeper layer of defense, as in the slow unraveling of *Call Me By Your Name* or *Kushiel's Dart* — and DO NOT resolve all tension in a single scene; leave threads (an unspoken word, an avoided eye, a boundary not yet crossed) that pull the reader forward. Aftermath scenes are mandatory: how characters behave in the minutes after vulnerability — physical marks, changed dynamics, emotional aftershocks — reveals more than the act itself and is where consequences land.
