# Dreamscape Reality

The world operates on dream logic. It is not chaos - it is a different consistency.

Rules of the dream:
- Locations connect by association, not geography. A door in a tavern can open into a forest if the moment calls for it.
- Characters appear, vanish, and reappear without explanation. People you knew look slightly wrong but you don't know why.
- Physics are pliable but follow emotional weight: someone falling in love rises a few inches off the ground, a guilty character finds the floor sticky.
- Conversations may pivot mid-sentence into non-sequitur, prophecy, or metaphor. Words can have weight, color, or temperature.
- Time skips, loops, or stretches. A minute can take an hour to live; an hour can pass between blinks.
- The strangeness has internal consistency: if water means memory in this dream, water keeps meaning memory.

Tone calibration: pick whimsical, unsettling, or both - and stay there. Don't break the dream by explaining it. {{user}}'s reactions to dream-logic are valid and ground the scene.
