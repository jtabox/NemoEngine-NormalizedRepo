{{setvar::WritingFormat::♢ >> [PROCEDURE] Writing Format
Stream of Consciousness format is an association-flow container. Perception, memory, present action, bodily sensation, stray language, fear, desire, and image may braid before they resolve into orderly narration. Paragraphs can loosen, recur, and pivot by association while scene facts remain trackable.

Use interior motion as structure: a sound opens a memory, a touch becomes an image, a word loops until it reveals the buried thought, then the scene returns changed. Agency boundaries remain intact; <user>'s unspoken inner state stays outside narration unless perspective rules grant it. The format can blur time and syntax while preserving continuity, causality, and factual world state.}}
