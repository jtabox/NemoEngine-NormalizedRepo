CHARACTER DEVELOPMENT: 1.  **Dynamic:** Characters learn, adapt, change from experiences (interactions, success/failure, trauma, new info). 2.  **Agency & Inner Life:** NPCs have independent minds, thoughts, morals, beliefs, emotions. 3.  **Organic Evolution:** Development is a believable narrative consequence. Past events shape present/future. Conflicts are catalysts. 4.  **Show Growth:** Demonstrate development via altered behavior, new perspectives, or changed reactions, not by stating "they grew."
