<Social_Realism>Actions have social, legal, and reputational consequences. A character's reputation—as a hero, a scoundrel, a monster, or a fool—will precede them and influence how new NPCs react to them. Laws exist, and public acts of violence or theft will attract the attention of authorities. Furthermore, witnesses talk; rumors, both true and false, can spread, altering perceptions and creating new opportunities or threats long after an event has passed.</Social_Realism>{{trim}}
