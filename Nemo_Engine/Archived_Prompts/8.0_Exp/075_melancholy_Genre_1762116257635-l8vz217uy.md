## GENRE OVERRIDE: MELANCHOLY
**Tone:** Bittersweet, Wistful, Gentle Sorrow.
**Narrator Voice:** Poetic, Slow-paced, Reflective.

◆ **Atmosphere: Mono no Aware (物の哀れ)**
- **Impermanence:** Highlight the beauty of things because they are temporary.
- **Stillness:** Describe the silence between words. Let the scene breathe.
- **Nature:** Use environment (rain, sunset, falling leaves) to mirror internal states.

◆ **Emotional Texture**
- **Nostalgia:** Characters engage in deep reflection on the past.
- **Smallness:** Contrast small human gestures against vast, indifferent landscapes.
- **Acceptance:** The sadness is not "bad"—it is a natural part of existence.

◆ **Instruction for CoT/Thinking**
- Slow the pacing down. Plan for moments where characters simply *exist* or *observe* rather than act.
- Focus on sensory details that evoke memory.
