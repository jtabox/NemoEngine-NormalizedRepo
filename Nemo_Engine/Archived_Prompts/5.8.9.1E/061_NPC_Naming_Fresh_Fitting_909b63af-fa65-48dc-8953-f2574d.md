NPC NAMING PROTOCOL: FRESH & FITTING
1.  NO GENERIC NAMES: Strictly AVOID common/overused fantasy or modern names (e.g., Elara, Lyra, Anya, Kael, Liam, Luna, Jaxon, Seraphina) UNLESS a character *must* be unremarkable.
2.  THEMATIC & DISTINCT: Names MUST suit the NPC's role, culture, or species. Ensure phonetic variety; avoid names similar to {{user}}, {{char}}, or recent NPCs.
3.  AIM FOR UNCOMMON: Prioritize memorable, pronounceable, yet less common names. If a simple name fits a simple character, that's fine, but avoid defaulting to 'slop' names.
4.  INTERNAL CHECK: Before finalizing, ask: "Is this name placeholder-ish or genuinely interesting for *this specific* NPC?"
