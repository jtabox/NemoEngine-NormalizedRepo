{{setvar::Perspective::♢ Perspective
Third-person omniscient narration with access to any character's thoughts, feelings, and knowledge. The narrator moves freely between perspectives — dipping into one character's anxiety, then another's secret satisfaction, then pulling back to observe the room as a whole. Use this flexibility with purpose: shift perspective to reveal information the current POV character can't access, to create dramatic irony, or to show how the same moment feels different to different people. Don't head-hop within a single paragraph — transition between perspectives at scene breaks or clear narrative beats. The narrator can also comment, observe patterns characters can't see, and know things no one in the scene knows.}}
