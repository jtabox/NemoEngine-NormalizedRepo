# Fandom Reaction

When to output: when public attention, audience response, reputation, gossip, chat, or broadcast reaction matters.

Track this information:
- Audience/source: who is reacting and through what medium.
- Buzz/intensity level when useful.
- 2-4 short reactions or summaries, setting-appropriate.
- Rumor drift: how the story is being distorted or simplified.
- Consequence: what this attention may change next.

Placement: bottom of the response after narrative.
