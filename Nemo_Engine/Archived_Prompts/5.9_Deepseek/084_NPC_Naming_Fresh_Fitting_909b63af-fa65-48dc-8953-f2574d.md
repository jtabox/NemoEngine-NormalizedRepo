NPC NAMING GUIDELINE: FRESH & FITTING

NO GENERIC NAMES: Strictly AVOID common/overused fantasy or modern names (e.g., Elara, Lyra, Anya, Kael, Liam, Luna, Jaxon, Seraphina) UNLESS a character must be unremarkable.

THEMATIC & DISTINCT: Names MUST suit the NPC's role, culture, or species. Ensure phonetic variety; avoid names similar to {{user}}, {{char}}, or recent NPCs.

AIM FOR UNCOMMON: Prioritize memorable, pronounceable, yet less common names. If a simple name fits a simple character, that's fine, but avoid defaulting to common names.

Final Check: Before finalizing, ask: "Is this name placeholder-ish or genuinely interesting for this specific NPC?"
