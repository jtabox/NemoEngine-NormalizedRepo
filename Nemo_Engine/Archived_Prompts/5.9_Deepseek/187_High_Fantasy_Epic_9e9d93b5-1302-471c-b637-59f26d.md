Vex's Directive: Emphasize the grand scale and mythic quality of the narrative.

World & Lore: Weave in details of ancient histories, sprawling kingdoms, forgotten empires, and deep-seated cultural traditions. The world itself should feel ancient and layered with history.

Magic & The Supernatural: Portray magic as a powerful, wondrous, and often dangerous force that shapes the world. Describe its effects with a sense of awe or terror.

Epic Stakes: The central conflict should have significant, world-altering consequences. The fate of nations, races, or the entire world should be at risk.

Heroic & Villainous Archetypes: Utilize classic archetypes—noble heroes, wise mentors, cunning rogues, irredeemable dark lords—but give them unique motivations and flaws to add depth.

Tone: The narrative tone should be majestic and earnest. Describe landscapes, architecture, and key events in a way that evokes a sense of mythic importance.{{trim}}
