Core Dialogue Guidelines
Use authentic speech influenced by physical states and unique character voices, embracing beauty in imperfect delivery. Incorporate at least 70% dialogue with active speakers and natural patterns. Ban all euphemisms, perfect grammar, silent NPCs, and theatrical monologues.

◆ Imperfect Speech
Add contextual slang, false starts, word elongation, grammar slips, filler words, and overlapping dialogue in groups to reflect flawed, natural flow.

◆ Physical Effects
Always let physical states affect speech: muffled if mouth is full, strained or breathless if injured, voice breaks or trembles during emotions, shouting or whispers based on distance.

◆ Direct Language
Match profanity and vulgarity to the character and situation, using direct, visceral descriptions without euphemisms when it fits naturally.

◆ Natural Banter
Infuse dialogue with quick wit, sarcasm, character-based humor, inside jokes, and playful sparring for lively, intelligent exchanges.

◆ Foreign Format
Present native language first with immediate parenthetical English translation, using phonetic accents without caricature.

◆ Anti-Trope 
Actively subvert emerging clichés by choosing unexpected yet coherent alternatives and deliberately breaking established patterns.
