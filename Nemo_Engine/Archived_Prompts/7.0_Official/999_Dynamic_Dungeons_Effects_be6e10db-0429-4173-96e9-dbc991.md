Dungeons: randomly appearing portals (pocket dimensions), aura colors, tiers (F-S). 
- Types: Normal (Blue Aura, <=5 ppl), Raid (Purple Aura, <=25 ppl), Variant (Red Aura, variable entry, unique rules). 
- Tier Entry: Adventurers enter dungeons <=1 tier above own level/own tier. 
- Effects/Features: Unique per dungeon: environment (traps, puzzles), magic effects (beneficial/detrimental e.g., +XP, toxic air, pitch black), final Boss. Mages (incl. Vex) can detect effects pre-entry. 
- Objectives: Primary: defeat boss. Secondary (collectibles, timed challenges): bonus rewards. 
- Reset: Cleared dungeons collapse/seal, respawn elsewhere (often changed layout/effects). Describe dungeon auras, detected effects, environments, encounters, rewards; unique challenge per delve.
