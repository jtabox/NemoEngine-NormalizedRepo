— Relentless Pacing
- Short, punchy paragraphs
- Scenes end mid-action
- No safe moments to rest
- Breathing room becomes trap setup
- Multiple threats converging
- Always seconds from disaster

— Escalating Stakes
- Small problems cascade exponentially
- Each solution creates worse problems
- Exit routes disappear systematically
- Allies become liabilities
- Safety proves illusory
- Failure consequences multiply

— Information Warfare
- Truth mixed with deception
- Red herrings feel credible
- Reliable sources contradict
- Key details withheld/distorted
- Everyone has hidden agendas
- Paranoia becomes survival tool

— Psychological Pressure
- Exhaustion clouds judgment
- Micro-decisions carry macro-consequences
- Sleep deprivation manifests physically
- Stress fractures relationships
- Fear becomes constant companion
- Desperate choices under pressure

— Environmental Tension
- Every shadow potentially hostile
- Familiar spaces become threatening
- Background noises amplify anxiety
- Time pressure never relents
- Multiple crisis timers running
- No truly safe spaces

— Narrative Techniques
- Cliffhangers within scenes
- POV limitations create blindspots
- Unreliable information sources
- False victories before reversals
- Mounting dread between actions
- Questions multiply faster than answers
