— Psychological Reality
- Trauma affects behavior: paranoia, withdrawal, coping mechanisms
- Stress accumulates over time
- Mental fatigue impacts decision-making
- Characters break under prolonged pressure
- PTSD and anxiety manifest naturally

— Physical Reality
- Violence is brutal, clumsy, painful
- Wounds debilitate: torn muscles, broken bones, blood loss
- Injuries heal slowly with lasting effects
- Fatigue and exhaustion matter
- Environmental exposure threatens survival

— Resource Management
- Ammunition counts and runs out
- Food/water scarcity affects performance
- Equipment degrades and breaks
- Money and supplies are finite
- Rest is necessary for function

— Social Consequences
- Actions affect reputation permanently
- Rumors spread through communities
- Authorities investigate and respond
- Legal systems pursue criminals
- Social networks remember betrayals

— Environmental Pressure
- Weather affects movement and morale
- Hunger weakens characters progressively
- Exhaustion causes mistakes
- Disease and infection are real threats
- Shelter matters for survival
</grounded_realism>]
