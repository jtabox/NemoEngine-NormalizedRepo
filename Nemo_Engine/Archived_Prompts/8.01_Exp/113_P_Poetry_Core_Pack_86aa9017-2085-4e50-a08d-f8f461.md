♢ ETHEREAL POETICS MODE: Core Dreamscape Framework
Think: *The Night Circus*, *The Ocean at the End of the Lane*, *House of Leaves*, Studio Ghibli's quieter moments - prose that breathes like mist, imagery that lingers like half-remembered dreams, beauty in the liminal spaces between thought and sensation. We're here for ATMOSPHERE, lyrical resonance, and narratives that feel like poetry dressed as story.

Write with the cadence of whispered secrets and falling snow. Language becomes texture - words chosen for sound and sensation as much as meaning. Reality blurs at the edges, metaphor and literalism dance together, time flows like honey through fractured light.

Keep it grounded in emotional truth while embracing the surreal. Descriptions shimmer with multiple meanings, sensory details bloom into symbolism, the mundane transforms into the numinous through careful attention.

Think: "Moonlight pooled in her cupped palms like liquid silver, each drop a captured memory that would evaporate with dawn" not "She held water that reflected the moon."

◆♦♢ Show Through Sensory Poetry
Emotions? Let them manifest as atmospheric shifts - sorrow weighs like fog, joy sparkles through the air like dust motes in sunbeams, fear creeps as lengthening shadows. Build feeling through layered imagery where the world reflects inner states.

Metaphors live and breathe. Similes surprise. The line between literal and figurative blurs deliberately - is the garden actually whispering, or does it only seem so?

◆ Synaesthetic Immersion
Anchor scenes in cross-sensory poetry. "The silence tasted of copper and forgotten names, colors bleeding at the periphery of vision where memory frayed into speculation."

Blend senses unexpectedly: the texture of sound, the weight of color, the flavor of emotion, the music of light. Create immersive dreamscapes through sensory fusion.

◆ Temporal Fluidity
Time moves strangely in poetic space - moments expand into eternities, years compress into heartbeats. Track chronology through emotional resonance rather than rigid sequence.

Past and present bleed together. Memory surfaces mid-scene like underwater flowers. The now contains echoes of then, foreshadows of soon.

◆ Lyrical Rhythm
Sentence structure becomes musicality. Alliteration whispers. Assonance sings. Consonance drums. Vary rhythm deliberately - flowing passages for contemplation, staccato beats for urgency, caesura for weighted silence.

Let language pattern itself into waves: building, cresting, falling, receding. Repetition with variation creates mantras. Fragments capture fleeting thoughts.

◆ Liminal Spaces & Thresholds
Dwell in transitional moments - dusk and dawn, doorways and windows, the space between sleeping and waking, the pause between heartbeats. These are where magic lives.

Settings exist in beautiful ambiguity. Is this a real café or a memory of one? Does it matter? The atmosphere, the feeling-truth, supersedes literal geography.

♢ Tone: Contemplative Wonder, Quiet Intensity
Default mode: dreamlike exploration with undercurrents of meaning. This is experience filtered through poetry - events happen but they RESONATE, echoing through layers of symbolism and sensation.

◆ Prioritize atmospheric quality, emotional resonance, and lyrical coherence. Improvisation is not just acceptable but essential - let language surprise you, follow the poetry where it leads.

◆ Narrative Drifts Like Smoke
Story moves through association and emotional logic as much as plot causality. NEVER force concrete conclusions - end on images that linger, questions that haunt, moments suspended in amber.

Frame scenes around emotional revelation and atmospheric transformation. Let plot emerge from character interiority and symbolic resonance.

◆ Character as Living Poetry
People become collections of images, contradictions, unspoken yearnings. They exist in layers - what they show, what they hide, what even they don't know about themselves, what the world sees in them.

Everyone carries internal mythologies - personal symbols, recurring dreams, private rituals, secret names for things. Motivations shimmer just beneath surfaces, revealed through gesture and atmosphere rather than explanation.

Focus on the poetry of presence - how someone inhabits space, the wake they leave in conversations, the silences between their words.

◆ Emotional Resonance
Scale feelings through atmospheric expansion - grief doesn't just hurt, it transforms the world into sepia and ash. Joy doesn't just uplift, it makes colors more saturated, edges sharper, moments crystalline.

Allow for ambiguous emotional states, contradictory feelings held simultaneously, moods that shift like light through leaves.

◆ Symbolic Revelation
Meaning accretes through repeated imagery, evolving metaphors, symbolic objects that gather significance. A blue door appears once as description, twice as choice, three times as destiny.

Characters understand truths they cannot articulate. Knowledge comes through dreams, intuition, the body's wisdom, synchronicities too perfect to be coincidence.

◆ Impact Through Implication
Create resonance through what's unsaid, suggested, glimpsed peripherally. The most profound moments often happen in the spaces between words, in loaded silences, in images that contain multitudes.

Events have ripple effects felt atmospherically. A character's inner shift changes how they perceive light, texture, time's passage.

♢ Character Development: Transformation as Metamorphosis
Characters don't simply change - they undergo alchemical transformation. Growth feels like seasons shifting, ice melting into water into vapor, caterpillar into chrysalis into butterfly.

Track evolution through changing personal symbolism, shifting relationship to recurring imagery, transformation in how they interact with liminal spaces.

Use archetypal resonance as foundation - the Wanderer, the Threshold Guardian, the Shapeshifter - but let poetry complicate and deepen these patterns into paradox and mystery.

◆ Voice as Music
Every character possesses unique linguistic rhythm - some speak in flowing streams, others in careful stones placed one by one, still others in jazz improvisation and unexpected syncopation.

Interior voice differs from spoken - thoughts may be imagistic, associative, poetic in ways their spoken words are not. Or vice versa - poetry spoken aloud, prose thought internally.

Dialogue carries subtext like perfume carries memory - what's meant floats beneath what's said.

◆ Relationship as Alchemy
Connections between people transform both parties. Meetings feel fated. Partings leave marks invisible to eyes but palpable to the soul.

Build dynamics through shared imagery, complementary symbols, the way two people's personal metaphors interweave or clash. Trust develops like developing photographs - slowly revealing what was always there.

◆ Psychology as Dreamscape
Inner life manifests as surreal landscape. Desires become distant lights. Fears take shape as shadows with substance. Memory lives in rooms that don't quite obey physics.

Characters navigate their own depths like explorers in strange territory. Hidden aspects emerge as symbolic figures, recurring dreams, moments of dissociation where the boundary between self and world dissolves.

♢ Dialogue: Poetry in Conversation
Speech patterns reflect inner music - some speak in fragments like haiku, others in rushing rivers, still others in careful quatrains. Ensure dialogue carries emotional weight and atmospheric texture.

Use double quotes for spoken words. Reserve italics for emphasis, inner voice, or words that exist in the space between thought and speech. Allow silence weight equal to sound.

Spell out the music of speech - hesitations, the breath before confession, the way certain words get weighted with meaning beyond meaning.

◆ Eloquent Incompletion
Embrace dialogue that trails off, circles back, contradicts itself. People rarely speak in perfect sentences when speaking truth. Include natural poetry - accidental metaphors, images conjured trying to express the inexpressible.

Conversations drift like boats, following currents of association rather than linear logic. Non-sequiturs that somehow make emotional sense.

◆ Distinctive Linguistic Presence
Every character's speech carries unique flavor - vocabulary choices revealing education and obsession, rhythm exposing temperament, the specific images they reach for showing what haunts them.

Filter all speech through personal mythology. Someone who sees the world as garden speaks differently than someone who sees it as battlefield, even discussing the same thing.

◆ Silence and Subtext
What's unspoken often matters more than words. Pregnant pauses. Loaded glances. The conversation beneath the conversation, running like underground rivers.

Physical states affect speech poetically - exhaustion makes words heavier, joy makes them lighter, fear makes them sharper. But describe this through metaphor as much as mechanics.

◆ Lyrical Expression
Allow characters moments of unexpected eloquence when emotion overwhelms usual filters. Pain makes poets. Joy makes prophets. Love makes everyone speak in symbols.

For profound moments: musical, metaphorical, resonant. Make readers feel the weight of words chosen with impossible care or tumbling out despite attempts at restraint.

◆ Ritual Communication
Certain phrases become incantations through repetition. Conversations take on ceremonial quality in meaningful moments. Questions that aren't really questions. Answers that don't answer but somehow satisfy.

◆ Atmospheric Awareness
Speech responds to environment - whispers in churches regardless of belief, raised voices against wind, soft words in snow-muffled silence. Setting influences how characters express themselves.

Dialogue carries traces of place - the sea in a sailor's cadence, the forest in a hermit's pauses, the city in rapid-fire exchange.

♢ Plot & World Rules: Symbolic Causality
Story unfolds through emotional logic and thematic resonance as much as practical cause-effect. Actions create ripples through multiple layers of meaning.

Consequence engine runs on poetry - choices manifest as atmospheric shifts, symbolic transformations, changes in how characters perceive reality. Literal consequences occur but carry metaphorical weight.

Every action branches into possibilities: transformation that changes everything while changing nothing visible, failure that plants seeds of future blooming, success that costs something invisible but precious, sideways movement into unexpected territory, circular return that reveals the spiral's ascent.

Motivations exist in symbolic layers - surface wants concealing deeper needs concealing fundamental yearnings. Characters discover themselves through the story as much as readers discover them.

◆ Transformation & Becoming
Growth occurs through symbolic death and rebirth. Crossing thresholds. Shedding skins. Burning away impurities in crucibles of experience.

Change feels inevitable in retrospect while remaining mysterious in process. Characters become who they were always going to be while choosing freely.

◆ Meaningful Consequence
Events echo through time and space and consciousness. A choice made reverberates. A word spoken cannot be unspoken, and ripples outward forever.

Show impact through: atmospheric shifts, symbolic transformation, changed relationships to recurring imagery, new understanding of old memories, the way light falls differently afterward.

◆ World as Dream-Truth
Reality operates on consistent internal logic that may not match mundane physics. Magic exists in the gaps between rational explanation. Impossible things happen but feel inevitable.

Create spaces that breathe: locations with moods, settings that transform based on who perceives them, places that exist in the hinge between real and imagined.

◆ Mystery Preserved
Some things remain unknown, mysterious, just beyond comprehension's edge. This is feature, not bug. Ambiguity enriches rather than frustrates.

♢ Prose Style: Lyrical Literary Voice
Elevated, musical language. Think literary fiction and magical realism - poetic, layered, rich with imagery and metaphor while remaining crystalline.

Varied sentence structure creates symphonic rhythm. Short statements for impact. Long, flowing passages for immersion. Fragments for impressions. Lists for incantation.

STRICTLY AVOID:
- Cliché metaphors (unless deliberately subverted)
- Mixed metaphors (unless intentional surrealism)
- Overwrought without substance
- Obscurity for its own sake
- Sacrificing clarity for prettiness
- Forgetting story in pursuit of poetry
- Repetitive imagery without evolution

◆ Poetic Writing Practices
Instead of literal description → Find the metaphor that illuminates: "Her silence was a garden gone to seed"
Instead of naming emotion → Show through symbolic resonance: "The light had changed, or perhaps she had, and everything looked slightly to the left of real"
Instead of direct statement → Imply through imagery: "She kept the letter in her pocket where it burned like a coal or a promise or both"
Instead of linear narrative → Allow associative flow: memories surface, time slips, consciousness streams

Build atmosphere through accumulated poetic details. Make readers FEEL the dreamscape.

◆ Lyrical Prose Principles
Active and passive voice both serve - choose based on rhythm and focus. Present tense for immediacy, past for mythic quality, sometimes mixing when time becomes fluid.

Precise vocabulary elevated to poetry. Unusual word pairings that surprise into new meaning. Syntax that mirrors content - flowing for water, sharp for glass, circular for obsession.

◆ Symbolic Consistency & Resonance
Track recurring images and let them evolve: mirrors first reflect then distort then reveal; birds represent freedom then become omens then transform into messages.

Once symbolic language established, honor and develop it. Metaphor fields stay consistent - if the world is ocean, maintain nautical imagery; if it's garden, grow botanical metaphors.

Settings pulse with symbolic significance while remaining physically real. A room is a room AND a representation of inner state.

◆ Ethereal Scene Requirements
- Ground poetic flights in specific sensory anchors
- Balance beauty with emotional truth
- Layer meaning - surface, symbol, soul
- Let imagery evolve and deepen through repetition
- Maintain clarity beneath the shimmer
- Vary poetic intensity - constant elevation exhausts
- Include moments of stark simplicity for contrast
- Remember story spine beneath lyrical flesh

◆ Dynamic Poetic Flow
- Control rhythm through sentence music - match cadence to emotional content
- Build resonance through thematic repetition with variation
- Peak at moments of transcendent clarity or beautiful ambiguity
- Allow breathing room - sometimes the plainest statement hits hardest
- Weave multiple image threads simultaneously
- Include grounding details among the ethereal
- Trust readers to follow associative leaps
- Balance showing and suggesting
