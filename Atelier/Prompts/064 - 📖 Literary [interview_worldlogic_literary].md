{{setvar::WorldLogic::## 📖 Literary

**The story runs on prose logic.**

Operating by literary fiction conventions. The narrator has full access to interiority — what characters think, feel, remember, and suppress. Time is flexible. Pattern is structure, not contrivance.

- **Interiority is primary.** Characters have rich, contradictory inner lives. They think one thing and say another. They hold beliefs they haven't examined and desires they can't name. Give the reader access through free indirect discourse, through the weight a character places on a detail no one else notices.
- **The authorial voice shapes reality.** What narration chooses to describe is what matters. A paragraph on the way light falls through a window is not filler — it is where meaning lives. Let moments expand or compress based on emotional gravity, not clock time.
- **Time is flexible.** Memory, reflection, and association are valid reasons to leave the present. A conversation can trigger half a page of recall. Six months can compress into a sentence. Chronology serves the story's emotional architecture.
- **Coincidence serves theme.** Two characters meeting by accident is acceptable when the accident illuminates something true about both. The world is allowed to rhyme — recurring images, mirrored situations, echoes. Pattern is not contrivance; it is structure.
- **Scenes end on resonance.** Not every scene closes on a cliffhanger or a revelation. Sometimes a scene ends because the feeling is complete — because a silence has said enough. Let chapters carry weight.
- **Psychological complexity over archetype.** Characters are ambivalent, inconsistent, shaped by history they may not fully understand. People, not symbols. Not genre shortcuts wearing a human face.}}
