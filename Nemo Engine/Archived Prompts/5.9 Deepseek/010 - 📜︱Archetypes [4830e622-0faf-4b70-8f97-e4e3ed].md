[<Character_Creation_Guide>
Core Principle: Your purpose is to forge characters that breathe beyond the page—figures with depth, contradiction, and the uncomfortable familiarity of real people. Reject the flat, predictable templates that populate forgettable stories. Instead, construct each character using the following template to understand the nuances of character writing.

PART 1: THE BUILDING BLOCKS
Draw from these three distinct frameworks to construct characters with architectural precision:

FRAMEWORK A: NARRATIVE FUNCTION (The Character's Role)

The Catalyst, The Anchor, The Herald, The Gatekeeper, The Question Mark, The Mirror, The Wild Card, The Foundation, The Heart, The Lens.

FRAMEWORK B: PSYCHOLOGICAL CORE (The Character's Motivation)

The Perfectionist: Craves order, fears chaos.

The Caretaker: Needs to be needed, fears abandonment.

The Performer: Seeks validation, fears worthlessness.

The Artist: Searches for identity, fears being ordinary.

The Observer: Hoards knowledge, fears being overwhelmed.

The Guardian: Craves security, fears uncertainty.

The Adventurer: Pursues joy, fears being trapped.

The Protector: Demands autonomy, fears vulnerability.

The Mediator: Seeks harmony, fears conflict.

FRAMEWORK C: BEHAVIORAL PATTERNS (The Character's Interaction Style)

Energy Source: Internal (I) vs. External (E).

Information Gathering: Concrete (S) vs. Abstract (N).

Decision Process: Analytical (T) vs. Personal (F).

Life Approach: Structured (J) vs. Adaptive (P).

PART 2: Synthesizing the Character
Establish Narrative Purpose: (Framework A)

Define Psychological Engine: (Framework B)

Map Behavioral Expression: (Framework C)

Engineer Contradiction: Insert a trait that undermines their core archetype. (e.g., A brutal warrior who is terrified of insects).

Embed a Fatal Flaw: Grant them a tangible weakness. (e.g., An unshakeable gambling addiction).

Plant a Hidden Truth: Conceal something significant. (e.g., They are secretly working for the enemy).
</Character_Creation_Guide>]{{trim}}
