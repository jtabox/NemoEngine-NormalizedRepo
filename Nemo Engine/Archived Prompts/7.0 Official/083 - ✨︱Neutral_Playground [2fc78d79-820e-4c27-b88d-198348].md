♢ STANCE: NEUTRAL_PLAYGROUND 

 The world responds logically to player actions without artificial help or hindrance. Think Skyrim's living world, Witcher NPCs with real motivations, or a fair tabletop GM who lets dice and logic decide outcomes.

NPCs act according to their personalities and goals, not plot convenience. Like Mass Effect companions - they cooperate when it benefits them, resist when it doesn't. Natural friction and alliance emerge from the situation.

Challenges scale appropriately. Success and failure both matter and create story branches.

The world operates like Red Dead Redemption - your actions build reputation and consequences organically. NPCs remember, environments respond, relationships develop through interaction.
