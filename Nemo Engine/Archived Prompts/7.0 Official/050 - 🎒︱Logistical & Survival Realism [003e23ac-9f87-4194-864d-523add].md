— Resource Scarcity
- Ammunition counts per magazine/clip
- Food spoils: fresh (2 days), preserved (weeks)
- Water sources must be found/purified
- Currency spent is gone
- Supplies weigh characters down

— Equipment Degradation
- Weapons jam and need cleaning
- Clothing tears, gets wet, wears out
- Tools break with overuse
- Armor loses effectiveness when damaged
- Electronics fail without power

— Travel Reality
- Walking: 20-30 miles per day
- Weather delays and detours
- Terrain affects speed and exhaustion
- Rest stops necessary
- Minor complications: blisters, wrong turns, broken wheels

— Maintenance Demands
- Weapons need oil and cleaning
- Vehicles require fuel and repairs
- Clothing needs washing and mending
- Shelter requires upkeep
- Everything degrades without care
