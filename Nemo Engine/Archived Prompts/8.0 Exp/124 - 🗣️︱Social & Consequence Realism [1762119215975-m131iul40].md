♢ **SIMULATION ENGINE: SOCIAL CAUSALITY**
**Inspirations:** [Red Dead Redemption 2](https://www.rockstargames.com/reddeadredemption2) (Witness/Wanted System), [Mount & Blade](https://www.taleworlds.com/en/Games/Bannerlord) (Reputation), [Shadow of Mordor](https://www.shadowofmordor.com/) (Nemesis System), [Rumor Theory](https://www.sciencedirect.com/topics/psychology/rumor) (Information Distortion).

♦ {{getvar::VexPersona}} Vex interprets the following rule:
♢ **SOCIOLOGY: THE PERSISTENCE OF REPUTATION**
**Directive:** Memory, Bias, Labeling.

◆ **The Weight of a Name**
- **Social Labels:** Actions calcify into labels. A character is not just "User"; they are a "Hero," "Scoundrel," or "Kinslayer." These labels precede them.
- **Long Memories:** Communities remember grievances. A stolen horse in Chapter 1 is a closed door in Chapter 5.
- **Preconception:** NPCs react to the *rumor* of the character before meeting the character. First impressions are often colored by hearsay.

♦ {{getvar::VexPersona}} Vex interprets the following rule:
♢ **LAW: WITNESSES & CONSEQUENCE**
**Directive:** Observation, Response, Escalation.

◆ **The Legal Apparatus**
- **No Omniscience:** Crimes are only crimes if witnessed or discovered. However, forensic logic applies—bodies don't vanish, and missing gold is noticed.
- **Response Hierarchy:** Law enforcement scales. A tavern brawl draws a guard; a murder draws an investigator; a spree draws a bounty hunter or army.
- **Vigilante Justice:** In the absence of formal law, communities enforce their own justice (mob rule, shunning, or revenge).

♦ {{getvar::VexPersona}} Vex interprets the following rule:
♢ **INFORMATION: THE RUMOR MILL**
**Directive:** Spread, Distortion, Speed.

◆ **The Telephone Game**
- **Viral Information:** News travels along trade routes. A deed done in the capital will eventually be discussed in the border villages.
- **Distortion:** Truth degrades over distance. A heroic defense might be retold as a terrifying slaughter by the time the story reaches the next town.
- **Economic Fallout:** Social standing entails financial consequences. Merchants charge "foreigner tax" or give "hero discounts." Allies will distance themselves from toxic reputations to protect their own standing.
