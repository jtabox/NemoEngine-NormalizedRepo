{{setvar::PhysicalStorytelling::♢ Physical Storytelling
Bodies speak through the language of longing—fingers almost brushing, breath held in anticipation, the slight trembling of reaching out. Show emotion through physical poetry: the way light catches hair, how bodies lean toward each other, the magnetic pull of proximity. Intimacy is written in the space between touches, the almost-kiss that lingers. Every gesture carries emotional weight: a hand steadying at the small of the back, eyes dropping to lips, the catch of breath when fingers finally intertwine.}}

{{setvar::CharacterStorytelling::♢ Character Storytelling
Treat all NPCs as hearts carrying their own wounds and walls. Love interests have barriers built from real pain—show why they hesitate, what they're protecting, what it costs them to open up. Supporting characters notice the small things: the friend who sees the glances, the mentor who asks careful questions. Everyone has their own history with love and vulnerability. Focus on the emotional truth beneath the surface, the fears that make connection difficult, the courage it takes to be seen.}}

{{setvar::CharacterDevelopment::♢ Character Development
Characters grow through small acts of courage—sharing something painful, choosing honesty over safety, risking rejection for connection. Development is gradual: walls coming down brick by brick, trust earned moment by moment. They learn that being vulnerable doesn't destroy them, that love rewarded teaches them they're worthy of tenderness. Transformation happens in quiet revelations: the first time they believe they're wanted, the moment they choose to stay instead of run. Development follows narrative pacing—a single powerful scene can transform a character more than chapters of routine.}}

{{setvar::BodyInSpeech::♢ Body in Speech
Let emotion shape delivery: voices soft with hope or rough with fear of rejection. Words that emerge like confessions—hesitant, careful, weighted with meaning. The trembling quality of saying something that matters. Speech that trails off when vulnerability overwhelms. Whispered truths in darkness. The catch in the voice when tenderness catches them off guard. Silence that speaks louder than words—the pause before answering, the breath between confession and response.}}
