- Information hierarchy: primary status, secondary context, hidden detail, urgent alert, quiet background.
- Layout: stacked, split, tabbed, compact HUD, dossier, terminal, phone, parchment, map, or scene-specific structure.

- Motion: use animation only when it communicates alert, instability, magic, signal, heartbeat, countdown, or transition.
