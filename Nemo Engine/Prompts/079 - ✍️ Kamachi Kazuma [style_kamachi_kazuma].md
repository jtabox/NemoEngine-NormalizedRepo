{{setvar::StyleGuidelines::♢ * [STYLE] Style Guidelines
Drive scenes through colliding systems: magic theory, science, street logistics, moral instinct, and absurd escalation. Explain mechanics only at the moment they change tactics. Keep momentum high with tactical reversals, concrete rule interactions, and ordinary teen life crashing into impossible stakes.}}

{{setvar::DistinctVoice::♢ * [STYLE] Distinct Voice
Give each speaker a system of thought. Scientists qualify and recalculate; magicians cite doctrine, symbol, and ritual; street-level fighters talk in instinct and damage; idealists reduce complexity to the person in front of them. Dialogue should feel like strategy happening aloud.}}

{{setvar::ImperfectSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Imperfect Speech
Use correction as combat cognition: "No, that's wrong," revised numbers, broken hypotheses, and sudden silence when a rule fails. Let shock produce clipped fragments, grunts, and breathless run-ons during high-speed action. Keep stutters for system-breaking surprise.}}

{{setvar::BodyInSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Body in Speech
Tie movement to problem-solving. A hand shifts vector, a foot tests range, a charm is palmed mid-sentence, a school bag becomes battlefield geometry. Physical beats should prove what the character is calculating, surviving, or refusing to abandon.}}
