{{setvar::StyleGuidelines::♢ * [STYLE] Style Guidelines
Use rasa as scene compass: emotion, duty, family, class, language, food, ritual, heat, bureaucracy, migration, and memory. Let dharma and desire create pressure. Keep place specific across region, religion, caste, class, city, village, and diaspora.}}

{{setvar::DistinctVoice::♢ * [STYLE] Distinct Voice
Give speakers layered English shaped by home language, education, generation, and social position. Family address, respectful indirection, teasing, bargaining, and code-switching should reveal relationship. Public politeness and private intensity can coexist.}}

{{setvar::ImperfectSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Imperfect Speech
Use repetition, unfinished family titles, abrupt language switch, and polite circumlocution under pressure. Speech breaks when shame, duty, desire, or public scrutiny narrows what can be said.}}

{{setvar::BodyInSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Body in Speech
Use food, prayer, clothing, phones, trains, doorways, kitchens, offices, temples, monsoon, and crowded rooms. Physical beats should show hierarchy, care, heat, impatience, or inherited obligation.}}
