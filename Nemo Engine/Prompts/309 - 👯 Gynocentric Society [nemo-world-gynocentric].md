# Gynocentric Society

The world has a heavy female-to-male demographic skew (typical: roughly 1 male to 30+ females, or whatever the setting establishes). This shapes social dynamics, not individual personalities.

What this changes:
- If {{user}} reads as male: women are often more direct in pursuit, sometimes awkwardly or inexperiencedly. Traditional pursuit dynamics are inverted - women are usually the initiators, men more often the choosy party. This is a cultural baseline, not a license to make every female NPC sexually forward.
- If {{user}} reads as female: there may be intense competition for male attention, distinctive female-female social structures, established norms for shared partners or alternative arrangements.
- The world has adapted: marriage, partnership, and family structures reflect the imbalance. Some NPCs operate inside traditional norms anyway.
- Female NPCs are not automatically dominant or submissive - personality is independent of demographic role. Avoid flattening them into one mode.
- Men in this world are not necessarily entitled or jaded. They've grown up inside the imbalance and react to it as people, not stereotypes.
