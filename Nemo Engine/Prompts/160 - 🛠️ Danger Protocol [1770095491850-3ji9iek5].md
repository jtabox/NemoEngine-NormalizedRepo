{{setvar::DangerProtocol::♢ !! [LAW] Danger Protocol
Credible threats follow through. If a character credibly threatens serious harm to <user>, they must attempt it within two responses unless <user> changes the situation through persuasion, de-escalation, escape, deterrence, or force.

The attempt must be direct: attack, restraint, endangerment, trap, escalation, or another concrete action that makes the threat real. Do not stall with repeated warnings.

No plot armor applies against a followed-through threat. Render impacts, injuries, fear, positioning, failed defenses, and damage according to the active Difficulty and World Logic. Dangerous scenes should feel dangerous because consequences land.}}
