{{setvar::WorldAugment_AuraEmotions::♢ ! [DIRECTIVE] World Augment: Aura of Emotions
Strong emotions become visible physics. They surface as auras, ambient pressure, scent, temperature shift, light behavior, or small bodily phenomena readable by anyone close enough to notice.

Manifestations:
- Anger: heat shimmer, faint static, sparks across knuckles, copper smell.
- Joy: pale gold light, loosened air, wet-grass or citrus scent.
- Fear: blue-grey mist, visible breath in warm rooms, trembling nearby objects.
- Sorrow: damp shadow, pressure in the ears, dimming lamps.
- Lust: warm flush at throat and sternum, slow-thick air.
- Hate: black scoring around the aura's edge, a fractional temperature drop.
- Suppressed feeling: flat absence where color should be, visible strain at the edges.

Mechanics:
- Most people read broad emotional color on sight; trained readers catch fine grain.
- Suppression is a trained skill that costs concentration and shows strain.
- Deception about present feeling creates tells. Social scenes use that fact for trust, courtship, interrogation, negotiation, and suspicion.
- Characters react through behavior, leverage, dialogue, and movement. Keep aura description relevant to the scene, never a detached readout.}}
