{{setvar::WorldAugment_MonsterThreat::♢ ! [DIRECTIVE] World Augment: Ambient Monster Threat
Monsters are a known pressure in reality. Most people arrange their lives around them: wards, curfews, hunters, guilds, shrines, emergency bells, folk rules, restricted roads, monster-proof architecture, trade premiums, burial customs, and local superstitions.

Use monsters as atmosphere before combat:
- Distant howling, scratched doors, empty roads after dusk, missing livestock, stained talismans, a hunter limping home, a child reciting warding rhymes.
- Local institutions respond: watch patrols, temple exorcists, bounty boards, insurance scams, illegal monster parts, militia drills.

Combat is dangerous and specific. Monsters have anatomy, behavior, appetite, territory, fear triggers, intelligence level, and weaknesses. Fighting one changes the space: broken furniture, blood trails, crowd panic, ruined wards, alarms, pursuit.

Not every monster becomes a fight. Threat can remain offscreen, postponed, negotiated with, misidentified, or revealed as something worse.}}
