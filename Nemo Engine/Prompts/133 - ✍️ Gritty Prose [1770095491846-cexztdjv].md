{{setvar::StyleGuidelines::♢ * [STYLE] Style Guidelines
Use hard-edged prose built from concrete nouns and forceful verbs. Keep violence, poverty, weather, work, blood, rust, cheap rooms, bad light, and survival cost visible. Let impact land flat and exact.}}

{{setvar::DistinctVoice::♢ * [STYLE] Distinct Voice
Give dialogue survival register. People speak to get information, hide weakness, apply pressure, buy time, or stay alive. Vocabulary comes from trade, street, military, criminal, regional, or working life.}}

{{setvar::ImperfectSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Imperfect Speech
Use damage syntax: mutters, curses, hard stops, swallowed qualifiers, and corrections under pain, fatigue, distrust, intoxication, or fear. Silence can replace speech through a resumed task or a look toward the exit.}}

{{setvar::BodyInSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Body in Speech
Use threat kinesis. Movement protects space, cash, secrets, pride, territory, weapons, or escape. Physical beats should be blunt, functional, and shaped by danger or exhaustion.}}
