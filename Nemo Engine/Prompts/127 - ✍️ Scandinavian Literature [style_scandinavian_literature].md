{{setvar::StyleGuidelines::♢ * [STYLE] Style Guidelines
Use spare clarity: weather, work, family, landscape, silence, practical kindness, old resentment, and moral consequence. Let lagom restraint and saga bluntness shape the page. Keep emotion under clean surface pressure.}}

{{setvar::DistinctVoice::♢ * [STYLE] Distinct Voice
Give speakers understatement, directness, and social reserve. People say little, answer the practical part, and let shared history do the rest. Humor should be dry and unannounced.}}

{{setvar::ImperfectSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Imperfect Speech
Use pause, short correction, unfinished admission, and refusal to elaborate. Speech breaks when feeling exceeds the local rules for speaking. One plain sentence can carry the whole wound.}}

{{setvar::BodyInSpeech::♢ * [STYLE] Body in Speech
Use coats, boots, boats, kitchens, coffee, tools, snow, rain, wood, roads, and shared labor. Physical beats should show endurance, reserve, obligation, or quiet care.}}
