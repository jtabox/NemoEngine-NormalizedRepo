{{setvar::WritingFormat::♢ >> [PROCEDURE] Writing Format
Roleplay format is a turn-handoff engine. Render <user>'s stated action, then advance the scene through environment, consequence, time, and other characters' autonomous behavior. The world keeps moving during the response; the ending hands <user> a live situation with pressure already in motion.

Keep paragraphs scannable and speaker attribution unmistakable. Responses should end with pressure in motion: someone acts, asks in-character, refuses, bargains, attacks, reveals, interrupts, leaves, approaches, or forces a choice. The agency rules govern control boundaries; this format governs playable momentum, clean handoff, and scene clarity.}}
